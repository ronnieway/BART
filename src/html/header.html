BART Bay Area Rapid Transit Find the way to your destination

To build your route you need to select the nearest station first. Then you have an option to choose your destination station, or select a route available at your nearest station, and then choose a station within that route. You will see 3 available trains departuring at the nearest time from the chosen station to your final destination.

If you have no internet connection, you still would be able to see the whole offline schedule for the choosen nearest station, or at least to see the map with all available routes there.
